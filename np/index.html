    हाम्रो बारेमा

    दलित समाज सेवा संघ (Dalit Society Service Organization – DSSO) एक गैर-सरकारी संस्था हो, जुन विशेष रूपमा पिछडा समुदायद्वारा नेतृत्व गरिएको छ र दलित अधिकार, गरिबी न्यूनीकरण, मानवअधिकार, नेतृत्व विकास, उद्यमशीलता विकास, वकालत, युवा सशक्तिकरण, भूमिसम्बन्धी अधिकार, सामाजिक विकास, न्याय र मर्यादाको सुनिश्चितताका लागि स्थापना गरिएको हो। यस संस्था २००० ईस्वीमा सिराहा जिल्लाको प्रशासनमा स्थापना गरिएको हो र २००१ ईस्वीमा सामाजिक सेवा परिषदसँग सम्बन्धित भएको छ। DSSO ले समुदाय र देशभरिको सामाजिक भेदभावपूर्ण अभ्यास र दुराचारको विरुद्ध दलित, महिला, जबरजस्ती श्रमिक र किशोर-किशोरीहरूलाई संगठन, सशक्तिकरण र आन्दोलन गर्दै आएको छ। स्थापना भएको समयदेखि नै DSSO दलित अधिकार, मानव तस्करी, आधुनिक दासत्व र जबरजस्ती श्रमको मुद्दामा काम गर्दै आएको छ। यस संस्थाले सरकारी लाइन एजेन्सीहरू, निर्वाचित प्रतिनिधिहरू, नागरिक समाजका संस्था, मुद्दा आधारित आन्दोलनहरू, ट्रेड युनियनहरू, विभिन्न राजनीतिक दलका सहोदर संगठनहरू, जिम्मेवार निकायहरू र सरोकारवालासँग सुदृढ समन्वय, सहकार्य र नेटवर्किङ, साथै गठबन्धन कायम गरेको छ।

      VMG Objectives Collaboration

    Vision, Mission and Goal (VMG) of the Organization:

    Vision:

    Creation of just and equitable Society without discrimination and exploitation in society and state.

    Mission:

    Organize and empower Madhesi Dalits for their leadership development to advocate on their fundamental rights issue to increase access in public service and resources.

    Goal:

    Contribute to promoting socioeconomic status, psychological boost up of Madheshi Dalits through awareness, rising on social discriminatory malpractice and empowerment for their voice and concern accessing public services and resources.

    Objective of the Organization:

    Objective (s) :

    • To advocate for the protection and promotion of human rights and social justice.
    • To raise the voice for the special rights and reservation of Madhesi Dalits, landless, poor and oppressed groups, Harawa-Charawa, migrant people, and adolescents.
    • To increase the access of Madhesi Dalits to government services and public resources.
    • To ensure the civil and political, as well as economic, social, and cultural rights of Madhesi Dalits.
    • To promote income generation opportunities for Madhesi Dalits and Harawa-Charawa communities for better livelihood options.
    • To promote quality education for children belonging to Madhesi Dalit communities.
    • To conduct awareness campaigns against caste-based discrimination and untouchability, dowry practices, early marriage, polygamy, and forced labor systems.
    • To enhance coordination and collaboration with government line agencies, policymakers, duty bearers, civil society organizations, media, lawyers, and community-based organizations.

    Through strategic partnerships and community-driven initiatives, DSSO continues to work toward building an equitable, inclusive, and dignified society for all. DSSO maintains strong coordination and collaboration with:

    • Government line agencies.
    • Elected representatives.
    • Civil society organizations.
    • Issue-based movements.
    • Trade unions.
    • Sister organizations affiliated with various political parties.
    • Duty bearers and relevant stakeholders.
